Overview

Boardrooms are critical spaces where strategic decisions, executive discussions, and important client meetings take place. A well-designed Boardroom AV solution integrates high-quality displays, professional audio systems, video conferencing technologies, and centralized control platforms to deliver a seamless communication experience.

Microlink designs intelligent boardroom environments that enable executives to conduct meetings efficiently, share content easily, and collaborate with remote participants. Our solutions also support chairman and delegate conference systems for structured discussions and meeting management.

Customer Challenges

  • Poor audio clarity and display quality during meetings
  • Difficulty managing discussions among multiple participants
  • Limited visibility of presentations and shared content
  • Difficulty connecting remote participants
  • Complex operation of multiple AV devices

Solution Provided by Microlink

  • End-to-end AV system design & integration
  • Large format displays or LED screens
  • PTZ video conferencing cameras
  • Ceiling or table microphones
  • DSP based audio systems
  • Wireless presentation systems
  • Centralized touch panel control systems
  • Chairman and Delegate conference system

Key Benefits of the Solution

  • Centralized Network Management
  • Improved Network Agility
  • Enhanced Network Visibility
  • Improved Security
  • Operational Efficiency
  • Scalable Infrastructure

Why Choose Microlink as a Solution Partner

  • Expertise in designing premium executive boardroom environments with seamless AV
  • integration
  • Experience with Chairman-Delegate conference systems, beamforming microphones, and DSP-
  • based audio processing
  • Integration with video conferencing platforms like Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and Cisco Webex
  • Intuitive touch panel control and automation for simplified meeting operation.
  • Clean rack integration, cable management, labeling, and documentation.
  • Reliable system performance for high-level corporate meetings.
